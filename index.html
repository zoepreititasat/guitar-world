Types of Guitars

Learn about different types of guitars and their unique features.

  • Acoustic Guitars
  • Electric Guitars
  • Bass Guitars
  • Classical Guitars

Acoustic Guitars

Acoustic guitars are known for their rich, natural sound and do not require amplification. They are often used in genres such as folk, country, and classical music.

Electric Guitars

Electric guitars require an amplifier to produce sound. They are versatile and used in many genres, including rock, metal, and blues.

Bass Guitars

Bass guitars provide the low-end sound in music and are essential in genres like rock, jazz, and funk. They come in both acoustic and electric varieties.

Classical Guitars

Classical guitars have nylon strings and a wider neck. They are primarily used for classical music, flamenco, and other fingerstyle genres.
